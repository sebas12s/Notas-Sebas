In the afternoon //tarde

PREPOSITIONS OF TIME
in, on & at
usamos on para indicar que un objeto está sobre otro, 
in para decir que uno está dentro de otro y at  para sólo hablar de la ubicación en general.

https://www.estudiaringles.online/in-on-at/

“I go to the park on Saturday at ten in the morning”
“I go to school on Monday at nine in the morning”
“I go to the gym on Wednesday at two in the afternoon”
“I go to the restaurant on Friday at eight thirty at night”
